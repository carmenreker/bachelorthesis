study controlled randomized controlled trial conducted October 2020 May 2021 Zanjan city , Iran , involving pregnant women mild moderate nausea vomiting . Ten health centers randomly selected , eligible participants identified national electronic identification system . Inclusion criteria specific Rhodes ' scores , gestational age , chronological age , literacy , contact availability . Exclusion criteria included pregnancy complications stressful events last three months . sample size determined pilot study calculated 30 participants per group , considering 95 % confidence level 80 % power . Participants allocated intervention control groups using randomized four-block method . intervention group received six sessions positive psychology counseling , control group received routine care . Data collected using Rhodes ' nausea vomiting severity scale , demographic questionnaire , pregnancy adjustment questionnaire . study outcome assessed coping patterns intervention . data analysis involved Kolmogorov-Smirnov test , Chi-square tests , independent t-tests , repeated measure ANOVA .
