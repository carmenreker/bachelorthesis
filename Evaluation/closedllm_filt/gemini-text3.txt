# # Summary Research Method controlled randomized controlled trial ( RCT ) designed investigate effectiveness positive psychology counseling coping pregnancy-related nausea vomiting ( NVP ) among pregnant women . * * Participants : * * * Pregnant women NVP ( Rhode 's score mild moderate ) * Gestational age : 6-10 weeks * Age : 18-35 years old * Literate phone number * Wanted pregnancy * Exclusion criteria : pregnancy complications , gastrointestinal disease , recent traumatic events * * Sample Size : * * * Calculated based pilot study previous research ( Mostafaei et al. , 2019 ) * Total sample size = 60 ( 30 per group ) * * Randomization : * * * Block randomization method four blocks ( AA , BB , AA , BB ) * Random allocation sequence concealed envelopes * * Intervention : * * * Positive psychology counseling based Tayyab Rashid 's protocol ( 6 sessions , 45-60 minutes ) * Delivered trained counselor following social distancing hygiene protocols * * Control Group : * * * Received routine care * * Outcomes : * * * Coping pregnancy pattern ( measured Pregnancy Adjustment Questionnaire ) * * Data Collection : * * * Sociodemographic questionnaire * Rhodes Nausea Vomiting Severity Scale * Pregnancy Adjustment Questionnaire ( measures preparation/planning , avoidance , positive-spiritual coping ) * * Data Analysis : * * * Statistical analysis performed using SPSS 16 * Tests include Chi-square , t-tests , repeated measures ANOVA , MANOVA * * Information : * * * study conducted October 2020 May 2021 health centers Zanjan , Iran . * detail intervention content provided separate table ( Table 1 , included summary ) .
